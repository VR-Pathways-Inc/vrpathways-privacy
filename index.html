VRBrain™ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Mood & Emotional Input: Users indicate how they’re feeling (e.g., stressed, burnt out, overwhelmed) before and after sessions to support self-reflection.
  • Interactive Session Responses: Users select CBT prompts and thoughts to shape their sessions.
  • Usage Data: We collect session completions, topic selections, and feature interactions.
  • Device Information: Non-personal technical data such as headset type and platform version.
  • Email Collection (Optional): For users who opt to receive worksheets via email. Collected only with consent.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize session content based on emotional input
  • Track user progress and feedback
  • Improve functionality and app performance
  • Deliver optional worksheets by email (with consent)

All session data is stored anonymously unless linked to an email address you provide.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services like:

  • Meta platform tools (for app analytics)
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) for data hosting, email delivery, and processing

4. Data Security

Data is transmitted and stored securely. Email addresses are stored separately and never shared.

5. Children’s Privacy

VRBrain™ is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your submitted data
  • Request deletion of your email address or records
  • Withdraw consent for worksheet emails

Contact: support@vrpathways.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. All updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
